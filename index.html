Transit Stops with Awnings in the Greater Seattle Area

Visualizing locations of stops for busses and trains in the Greater Seattle Area that have awnings. Awnings and other structures can help provide shelter from the weather, as well as protection late at night. Awning and other infrastructure such as lights can make a bus stop seem more complete and allow structure for riders to feel secure rather than just a sign in the middle of the road. However, it costs money to install this extra infrastructure, so many areas are underrepresented in terms of having these structures in place.

Sources: MapBox, King County GIS Open Data
